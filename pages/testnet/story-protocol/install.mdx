## <span style={{ fontSize: '20px', color: 'white' }}>The Role of Blockchain in the Creative Industry</span>
<div style={{ marginBottom: '20px' }}>
  Blockchain has three main features that make it relevant to the creative industry:
  1. Decentralization: By eliminating intermediaries such as agencies or distributors, creators can interact directly with their audiences. This increases profit margins and gives creators full control over their works.
  2. Security and Transparency: Blockchain technology ensures that every transaction or modification to a work can be publicly verified. This is crucial in preventing plagiarism and copyright infringement.
  3. Automation through Smart Contracts: Smart contracts enable automatic royalty payments to creators based on predefined rules. This reduces potential disputes and speeds up the payment process. <br />
